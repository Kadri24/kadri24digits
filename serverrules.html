Community Guidelines and Terms of Service

By joining and participating in our Discord server, you agree to comply with the following rules. Non-compliance with any of these rules may result in disciplinary action, including but not limited to, warnings, temporary suspensions, or permanent bans. We aim to create a respectful and safe environment for everyone.

Disclaimer: Rules are subject to change without notice. Continued participation in the server implies acceptance of any updated rules.

  1. Acceptance of Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines
    Before joining our server, you must agree to and comply with all of Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines. This includes age restrictions, no distribution of illicit software, and maintaining legal use of Discord's platform. Read Discord's TOS.

  2. Respectful Communication and Conduct
    We maintain a zero-tolerance policy for hate speech, harassment, bullying, discrimination, or disrespectful behavior of any kind. Treat all members with respect regardless of their identity or background.

  3. No Spamming or Excessive Use of Visual/Stylistic Elements
    Spamming, which includes the repeated use of emojis, special characters, images, and capital letters to seek attention, is prohibited. This rule applies to all text channels, voice channels, and private messages within the server.

  4. No Inappropriate or Violent Content
    Sharing explicit, violent, or disturbing content is strictly prohibited. This includes but is not limited to NSFW material, graphic images, self-harm content, and depictions of physical or verbal abuse.

  5. Protection of Personal Information
    Sharing personal information about yourself or others, including doxxing, is strictly forbidden. Do not share real names, addresses, phone numbers, emails, or any other personal information publicly or privately within the server.

  6. Respect Server Functions: No Spamming the Ticket System
    The ticket system is available to facilitate communication with moderators. Creating multiple tickets for the same issue or repeatedly spamming the system is strictly prohibited.

  7. Contact Moderators, Admins, and Owners Responsibly
    Direct messages to server staff should be limited to essential concerns. For all other queries or assistance, use the designated ticket system rather than direct messaging.

  8. Adherence to Server-Specific Activity Rules
    Follow all additional rules specific to certain channels or events as posted in the server. This includes restrictions on discussions, image posting, or gameplay rules as specified in those sections.

  9. Zero Tolerance for Malicious Activity
    Actions including but not limited to hacking, scamming, or any other form of fraudulent activity will result in immediate and permanent bans.

  10. No Advertising or Self-Promotion without Permission
    Advertising personal or third-party services, servers, or content without explicit permission is not allowed. This includes unsolicited private messages.

  11. Follow Voice Channel Etiquette
    Maintain appropriate behavior in voice channels, including avoiding excessive background noise, playing music without permission, or interrupting conversations.

  12. Staff Discretion for Moderation and Actions
    Moderators, Admins, and the Server Owner reserve the right to take any necessary actions to maintain a safe and positive community. If any behavior, language, or action is deemed harmful or disruptive, they may issue warnings, mute, or remove members from the server at their discretion.

  13. Do Not Directly Message Moderators, Admins, or the Owner
    Members are not permitted to contact moderators, admins, or the owner directly. For any concerns or assistance, please use the ticket system.
