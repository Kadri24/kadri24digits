Server Rules

When joining our Discord server, you must agree to our rules. If you do not agree with these rules, either do not join or leave if you're already in. We strive to make our server as safe and friendly as possible for everyone.

Warning: Be aware that new rules may be added.

  1. Accept Discord Terms of Service (TOS)
    Before joining Discord, and especially our server, you must agree to all of Discord's terms of service. Failing to do so will result in a ban from our server. Some examples from Discord's terms of service include not using their services if you are under 13 years old, and not distributing any illicit software. To check their terms of service (TOS), visit this website. https://discord.com/terms.

  2. No Hate Speech, Bullying, or Discrimination
    We do not tolerate any form of disrespect, bullying, slurs, or discrimination in our server.

  3. No Spamming, Excessive Use of Emotes, Special Characters, or Capital Letters
    Spamming is not allowed in our server. This includes excessive use of emojis, special characters, and capital letters to gain attention.

  4. No Sharing of Explicit or Violent Content
    Sharing explicit or violent content is not tolerated and will be punished. This includes content related to self-harm, fights, or NSFW material.

  5. No Sharing Personal Information or Doxxing
    We will never ask for your private information. Sharing personal information or using our server to share others' information will be punished. Doxxing is strictly prohibited and will result in punishment.

  6. Do Not Spam Our Ticket System
    Use the ticket system to communicate with our moderators. Do not create multiple tickets if you already have one open.

  7. Do Not Contact the Owner, Moderators and Admins
    Only contact them using tickets for anything related to the server and our products such as games for example.
