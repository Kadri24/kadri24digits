Slender The Eight Pages

Play Now

Story

Enter the dark and foreboding woods where an ancient evil awaits. Armed with nothing but a flashlight, you must collect all eight pages scattered throughout the forest while being hunted by the legendary Slender Man. Each page you find draws him closer, making your task increasingly dangerous.

The deeper you venture into the woods, the stronger his presence becomes. Your only tools are your wits, your flashlight, and your ability to run. But be warned - looking at him for too long will seal your fate. Can you collect all eight pages before he catches you?

Based on the iconic horror game that started a phenomenon, this faithful recreation brings all the tension and terror of the original to a new generation of players.

Features

Page Hunt

Search for the mysterious eight pages while managing your resources and maintaining your sanity.

Atmospheric Forest

Explore a detailed, fog-shrouded forest environment where danger lurks behind every tree.

Dynamic Tension

Experience increasing difficulty and tension as you collect more pages and Slender Man's presence grows stronger.

Resource Management

Carefully manage your flashlight usage and stamina while navigating through the darkness.
