Contact us faqs

Here are the most frequend answered questions we've got so far

Do you provide support?

Yes! You can contact us in different ways and here are some of them:
• Using this form
• Creating a ticket on our discord server
• Using our Twitter account

Note that you can use only one of them per requests. We are trying to respond every your requests as soon as possible so it may take some businesses days to respond your requests.

How will I get the answer

Your response will depend on the method you used to contact us:
• If you used our contact form (located at the top of this page), you will receive a response from our email address, kadri24digits@gmail.com. The response will be sent to the email you provided.
• If you utilized our Discord ticket system, the response will be provided within the corresponding ticket channel.
• If you contacted us via Twitter, you will receive the response within the chat.

We aim to reply to your inquiries as promptly as possible, but please be aware that it may take a few business days for us to respond.

Can I contact with offer requests or business related?

As our terms and conditions state, we do not accept your offers or any contacts related to the business field.

The answer is incorrect or there is an error, how can I contact back?

It depends on where you contacted us:
• If you contacted us through our contact form, you can reply to the answered email using the "reply" button and continue your case.
• If you used Discord, you can continue messaging us in the same channel.
• If you reached out to us on Twitter, you can continue the conversation in our chat.

Please note that we will respond through the same medium you initially contacted us on.
