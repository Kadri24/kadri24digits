Slendrina The Cellar

Story

Descend into the depths of terror in Slendrina The Cellar, where every shadow hides a secret and every corner could be your last. You find yourself trapped in a dark, oppressive cellar with only your wits and a flashlight to guide you. But you're not alone - Slendrina, the ghostly figure that haunts these halls, is watching your every move.

Search for a way to escape while uncovering the dark history behind Slendrina's existence. Each clue you find brings you closer to understanding the truth, but also draws her nearer. The cellar's maze-like corridors become a deadly game of cat and mouse as you try to survive long enough to find your way out.

Time is running out, and Slendrina's presence grows stronger with each passing moment. Will you uncover the truth and escape, or become another victim in her endless nightmare?

Features

Strategic Survival

Use your flashlight wisely and manage your resources as you navigate through the darkness of the cellar.

Complex Puzzles

Solve intricate puzzles and uncover hidden clues while avoiding Slendrina's deadly presence.

Haunting Atmosphere

Experience spine-chilling encounters and immersive horror in a meticulously crafted environment.

Dark Story

Piece together the tragic tale behind Slendrina's origin through scattered documents and eerie clues.
