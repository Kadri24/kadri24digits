Story

In this intense reimagining of the classic Eyes horror experience, you find yourself trapped in increasingly terrifying environments. As a daring thief searching for valuable items, you must navigate through dark corridors and haunted rooms while being stalked by Krasue, the vengeful Southeast Asian spirit.

But this time, the stakes are higher, and the horror is more intense. Krasue's supernatural abilities have grown stronger, and her hunting patterns have become more unpredictable. The environment itself seems to work against you, with new mechanics and challenges that will test your survival skills to their limits.

Each location holds its own dark secrets and unique horrors. What seems like a simple heist quickly descends into a fight for survival as you uncover the disturbing truth behind Krasue's presence and the cursed places she haunts.

Gameplay

Enhanced Horror

Experience a more intense and atmospheric horror gameplay with improved graphics, sound design, and scare mechanics.

Advanced Ghost Vision

Master the enhanced ghost vision system with new features and strategic elements to track and evade Krasue.

Expanded Environments

Explore larger, more detailed maps with interactive elements and dynamic events that affect gameplay.

New Mechanics

Discover new gameplay mechanics, items, and environmental interactions that add depth to the horror experience.
