Story

You are a desperate thief, drawn to an abandoned mansion by rumors of hidden wealth. Armed with nothing but your wit and courage, you break in hoping for an easy score. But this mansion holds dark secrets, and your simple heist is about to become a nightmare.

As you search for bags of money scattered throughout the building, you discover you're not alone. Krasue, a horrifying spirit that manifests as a floating head with internal organs hanging below, haunts these halls. This vengeful ghost, rooted in Southeast Asian folklore, protects the mansion's dark mysteries. With each bag you collect, Krasue becomes more aggressive, more determined to stop you.

What started as a burglary transforms into a terrifying game of cat and mouse. The deeper you venture into the mansion or hospital, the more you uncover about the tragic past through scattered notes and mysterious clues. Every corner could hide Krasue, waiting to strike. But beware - some secrets are better left buried, and greed comes with a deadly price.

Choose your hunting grounds wisely - explore the eerie corridors of the Classic Mansion or brave the abandoned Hospital's haunted halls. Each location offers unique challenges and tells its own dark tale. And this is just the beginning - new locations like the School and other terrifying venues are coming soon to expand the nightmare.

Gameplay

Survival Horror

Navigate through dark corridors and rooms while avoiding Krasue. Use your wits and stealth to survive - there's no fighting back against this supernatural entity!

Special Vision Ability

Utilize a unique "ghost vision" mechanic that allows you to temporarily see through the ghost's eyes, helping you track its movement and plan your escape routes.

Multiple Maps

Experience terror across various locations including the Classic Mansion, School, and Hospital, each with its own layout and challenges.

Dynamic AI

Face intelligent ghost behavior that adapts to your playing style, creating intense cat-and-mouse chases throughout the game.
